X-tra Time - TEDxKULeuven on air!

X-tra Time is the official podcast of TEDxKULeuven, an independently organized TEDx event at the University of Leuven. In this podcast, we invite multidisciplinary experts to talk about present societal issues and initiatives and, especially, about what the future may hold for us. As our events are held in and around the university campuses, we focus our conversations in particular on next-gen and youth activism.

Hosted by: Julius Schelstraete & Miklas Decock
Produced by: Baptist Devos
Music by: Paul Depuydt

Podcast guests

Jonathan Berthe

Jonathan is the founder and CEO of robovision, a company specialized in deep learning based machine vision and robot programming and is focused on the design, deployement, and development of complete automation projects.

Dirk De Wachter

Professor Dirk De Wachter is one of the most renowned practicing psychiatrists of Belgium and is asked a lot in the media for his expertise on human psychopathology. He is also a gifted teacher at KUL among others to the faculty of psychological sciences, and he is the author of several books among which bestselling ‘Borderline Times’.

Robin Kelchtermans

Robin Kelchtermans is a fellow student and was the vice-chairman of our student council ‘STURA’ and a member in the academic council of the KUL in the academic year of 2019-2020.

Bram De Ridder

Bram De Ridder is an historian within the modern area research group at KUL and he is the coordinator of CORVUS, a consulting platform that gives advice to government agencies among others based on what we can learn from our ancestors, form our collective past.
