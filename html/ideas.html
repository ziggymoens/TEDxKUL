Become a TEDx speaker | podcast guest!

Submit your ideas or expertise


Do you have a message, an idea, a revelation? Do you have an incredible story to tell, or just want to explain something in a new and refreshing way? This is the place to be to submit all of the above!


Whether it’s to share it on the stage or on our podcast, we will review your content or your request and will reach out to you within a week to provide feedback. Our goal is to give room for remarkable people with a remarkable message. Whether you are a seasoned speaker or someone who has never entered a stage before: we will coach you using the official TED-method of public speaking. And in the end employ this method in order to tame one of man’s biggest fear: stage fright.
