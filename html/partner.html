Become a partner and share our mission to spread ideas worth spreading!

Our event is only made possible through the support of companies, institutions, or even individual contributions. But our vision for our partners goes far beyond monetary support.

If you choose to become a TEDxKULeuven partner, you get access to:

  • The possibility to co-create meaningful side-events/ interactive booths with our organization during our projects throughout the year.
  • Behind the scenes networking opportunities: the chance to meet speakers, team members, get first look into our event planning and ideas.
  • Co-branding that solidifies the synergy of our collaboration in the minds of our target audience: students and academics.
  • All-partner events such as sponsor dinners, dress rehearsal attendance, etc.
  • Attendee benefits on the day of our events.
But we don’t want to limit a partnership conversation to these elements. We start with the companies, institutions, and people themselves and explore from zero and in an open way what we can offer. In this regard we try to be as creative and innovative as possible.

Our key partnership values:

  • Sustainability
  • Trust
  • Reciprocity
  • Openness
